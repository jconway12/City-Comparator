General Information

Each bar represents an 'urban area' from around the world. Bars are made up of ratings on a scale of 1 to 10 on 16 different categories (housing, cost of living, startups, venture capital, travel connectivity, commute, business freedom, safety, healthcare, education, environmental quality, economy, taxation, internet access, leisure and culture, tolerance, and outdoors), with 10 being the most desireable. This data is collected from the Teleport Api

Features and Use

Users can filter the data in a variety of ways through either the search bar or the side-panel. If a user wishes to see the data for a specific city, simply enter that city into the search bar and filter by city. Otherwise, 'all cities' allows the user to view all the available API data. Users can also filter by minimum ratings on each category by incrementing the sliders on the left and clicking the 'update criteria' button towards the bottom. By unchecking the checkboxes, users can remove certain criteria from the visualization. For example, by unchecking the housing criteria, the rating for housing will no longer be included in each urban area's bar representation. Colors coordinate between the side-panel and the bar graphs.
